Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

RecruitFlow is intended for users who are at least 13 years old. If you are under 18, you should use RecruitFlow only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.

1. Who We Are

RecruitFlow is a recruiting outreach assistant that helps student-athletes organize profile information, identify relevant school contacts, and generate editable outreach drafts.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, high school, graduation year, city, state, athletic position, athletic measurements, academic information, film links, strengths, notes, target schools, generated email drafts, and email history inside RecruitFlow.

If you sign in with Google, we receive basic Google account information such as your email address, name, Google account identifier, and profile picture if provided by Google.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide and improve RecruitFlow, authenticate users, save athlete profiles and email history, generate outreach drafts, recommend school contacts, troubleshoot issues, protect the service, and communicate with users about the service.

4. Google User Data

RecruitFlow uses Google sign-in for authentication. We use Google user data only to sign you in, identify your account, personalize your session, and save your profile and email history. RecruitFlow does not sell Google user data.

5. AI Providers and Service Providers

RecruitFlow may use service providers to host the app, store data, authenticate users, and generate draft text. These providers may process information as needed to provide their services to RecruitFlow.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate RecruitFlow, when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or as part of a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

7. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide RecruitFlow, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account information by contacting us.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

9. Children and Minors

RecruitFlow is not directed to children under 13, and users must be at least 13 years old. If we learn that we collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete it. Users under 18 should use RecruitFlow with parent or guardian permission.

10. Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, but some features may not work. You can disconnect Google sign-in, stop using the service, or request deletion of your account information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to: support@therecruitflow.com.
